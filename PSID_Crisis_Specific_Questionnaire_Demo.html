PSID Crisis Module - Combined Demo Questionnaire

This refreshed demo starts with a larger generic foundation so the questionnaire feels interview-ready even before the crisis-specific toggle starts. It then shows the active Pandemic / Natural Disaster add-on built from the Katrina and COVID items, plus alternate financial-crisis and war/conflict prototype banks.
